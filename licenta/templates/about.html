About Find My Spot

Find My Spot is a community-driven parking solution that helps drivers locate available parking spots in urban areas.

How It Works

1. Use our interactive map to find nearby parking spots
2. Add new parking spots you discover to help the community
3. Get directions to your chosen parking location

Features

• Real-time parking availability
• Interactive map with detailed spot information
• Community contributions and reviews
• Payment information (free or paid spots)
• Photo documentation of parking areas
• GPS-based navigation

Our Mission

We aim to reduce the time spent searching for parking, decrease traffic congestion, and improve urban mobility through community collaboration and modern technology.
