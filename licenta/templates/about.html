About Find My Spot

Find My Spot is a community-driven parking app that helps drivers locate available parking spots in an urban area.
With community collaboration and modern technologies we wish to improve urban mobility.
We aim to reduce the time spent searching for parking and reduce the traffic in the city.


How It Works

1. Use the interactive map to find nearby parking spots
2. Get directions to the nearest parking location
3. Add new parking spots you discover to help the community
4. Confirm other spots to help the community


Features

• Real-time parking availability
• Interactive map with detailed spot information
• Community contributions
• Payment information for each spot
• Images for certain spots
• GPS-based navigation
